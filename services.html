Our Services

Engineering & Design

We create tailored automation systems, guiding each project from the initial design to full deployment. Our expertise streamlines processes and ensures seamless integration with your existing infrastructure.

Robotics & Automation

Delivering cutting-edge robotic solutions, we specialize in robotic arm systems, collaborative robots (cobots), and intelligent automation platforms that enhance precision and operational efficiency.

Cutting & Welding Applications

Our robotic welding solutions deliver unmatched precision, consistency, and speed—reducing defects and improving workplace safety.

Software Solutions

We develop digitalization, telemetry, and remote monitoring software tailored to optimize industrial automation, including custom solutions for test benches and control platforms.

Commissioning & Technical Support

We provide full commissioning services to ensure your automation systems operate flawlessly, along with both remote and on-site technical support.

Service, Maintenance & Repair

Our comprehensive maintenance services include preventive maintenance, repair, reconditioning, retrofitting, and even ABB robot training to keep your operations running at peak performance.

Spare Parts

We supply new, reconditioned, and exchange parts for robot arms and controllers, along with consumables such as oils, batteries, and grippers.

Education & Training

We offer educational packages in robotics and automation technology, tailored to all brands with integration—empowering your team with the latest skills and knowledge.
